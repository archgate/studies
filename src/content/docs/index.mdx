**Archgate Studies** is an open research initiative investigating how software governance practices — particularly Architecture Decision Records — affect code review friction, team velocity, and decision quality.

All study materials, including analysis scripts and raw data, are published in the [source repository](https://github.com/archgate/studies) for transparency and reproducibility.

## Aims & Scope

Each study published here follows a rigorous, reproducible methodology:

- **Quantitative analysis** of real-world open-source repositories
- **Transparent methodology** with published scripts and assumptions
- **Data artifacts** for independent verification and peer review
- **Actionable proposals** — ADR packs and enforceable rules derived from evidence
